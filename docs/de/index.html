Mission

castletech exists to create software for dense urban living that enables community collaboration, property management, information sharing, and reasoned decision-making at every level.

With such tools in hand, communities can focus more on benefitting people to address the needs of residents effectively.

Philosophy

Empathy has been our driving force for millennia. It let us survive savannas full of predators by having us look out for one another. Harsh winters were overcome by cooperation during harvests and strategic provisioning during storms. Even today, with the zero-sum game of Capitalism devouring our one and only planet, we still find solace in organizations that achieve what we cannot do alone, for the future of our species â€“ to ensure we have a future at all.

We need to keep utilizing empathy in order to understand each other and work together to overcome the challenges facing us, and thrive despite them. One of those great challenges is a rapidly deteriorating environment due to global climate change caused by relatively recent human activity. In order to prevent further harm, one part of the solution is to use high-density, self-reliant cities to significantly reduce our ecological footprint and overall impact. We strive to achieve this goal with your help.

Our flagship product provides the platform required for the smooth operation of high-density housing projects such as apartments, condos, co-ops, and other Common Interest Developments. It's a system designed to facilitate easy collaboration amongst residents, as well as bringing transparency to the entire community regardless if it's self-governing or if there are third-party managers.

Going beyond property management, it's the neighborly app for bringing your community together.
