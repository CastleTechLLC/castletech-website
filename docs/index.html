Our Mission

One of the greatest challenges we face is a rapidly deteriorating environment due to global climate change caused by relatively recent human activity. In order to stop further harm, one part of the solution is to build high-density, self-reliant housing projects.

CastleTech exists to build humane tools for housing projects that enable considerate collaboration, the management and sharing of information, and informed decision-making at every level. With such tools in hand, these communities can become more friendly and efficient, giving them new abilities to address the needs of their residents effectively.
